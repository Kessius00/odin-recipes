Cinnamon Rum

Description

Warm up in winter with a glass of fabulously decadent cinnamon butter rum. It's the perfect tipple to enjoy at a festive gathering over the Christmas season.

Ingredients

  • 25 g butter
  • 2 tbsp golden caster sugar
  • 2 small cinnamon sticks
  • 200ml spiced rum

Steps

  1. Gently heat the butter, golden caster sugar and cinnamon sticks in a saucepan until the butter has melted and the sugar has dissolved.
  2. Stir in the spiced rum, then pour into four small heatproof glasses to serve.
